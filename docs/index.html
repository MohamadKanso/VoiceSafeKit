Simple workflow

Capture. Check. Protect.

01

Capture

Record your voice, upload a transcript, or paste text directly. Conversation mode lets you chain multiple turns and track cumulative risk.

02

Check

Detect PII, credentials, SSNs, IBANs, emotional distress, and coercion signals. Every finding shows a confidence score and inline highlight.

03

Protect

Get a redacted transcript, a detailed redaction map, and actionable guidance. Export the full result as JSON for downstream use.
